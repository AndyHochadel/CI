Who I Am

I'm Andy Hochadel, plant manager at Mi-Kin Creations. My focus is finding practical, low-cost ways to reduce waste, streamline workflows, and make the shop floor a better place to work. No theory for theory's sake — everything here has been implemented and tested in a real production environment.

I believe the best improvements come from the people doing the work. Most of the projects documented here started as a conversation with a machine operator or a frustration I noticed walking the floor. The goal is always the same: make the right thing easy to do and the wrong thing hard to do.

This site exists to document what we've done, share what worked (and what didn't), and give other small shops tools they can adapt. If something here saves you time or gives you an idea, that's a win.
